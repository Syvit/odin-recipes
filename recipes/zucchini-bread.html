Description

Oh boy. Zucchini Bread.

Ingredients

  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Zucchinis
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Nuts
  1. Begin by grating the zucchini. I use a food processor, but a box grater works well too. There is no need to wring out the liquid.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  3. In another large bowl, whisk the eggs with the sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the grated zucchini, melted butter and vanilla extract.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to liquid mixture.
  6. Add the nuts and currants.
  7. Divide the batter into two greased 8-1/2 x 4-1/2-in loaf pans.
  8. Bake for about 1 hour, or until tester comes out clean.
  9. Set the pans on wire rack and let cool about 10 minutes, then turn loaves out onto rack to cool completely.
